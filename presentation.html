The Problem

What Do We Need to Solve?

📸
Risk Analysis Taken
A technician scans the panel and generates a risk analysis at a specific physical location.
👷
Worker Opens the Report
Later, a worker opens the risk analysis on their phone before starting work on the panel.
✅
Verify Location Match
The app must confirm the worker is physically standing at the same location where the scan was taken.
⚠️
Warn if Mismatch
If the worker is too far away (>100m), show a clear warning before they proceed with work.
03 / 06
Option 1

GPS — Fused Location Provider

✅ Advantages
  • 📱Works on every Android device — no special hardware needed
  • ⚡Fast to implement — 2 to 3 hours of development
  • 🔋Battery efficient — uses network + GPS together
  • 🌐Works outdoors and indoors (with network assist)
  • 🎯5–50m accuracy — enough to verify building or site
  • 📦No extra dependencies — built into Google Play Services
❌ Limitations
  • 🏢Less accurate deep indoors (basements, concrete walls)
  • 📡Needs a moment to get first GPS fix
  • 🗺️Cannot distinguish which floor of a building
04 / 06
Option 2

ARCore — Augmented Reality

✅ Advantages
  • 📐Centimetre-level spatial tracking
  • 🎥Can overlay safety zones on live camera view
  • 🏢Works indoors without GPS signal
  • 🚀Future potential for AR safety overlays on panels
❌ Limitations
  • 📵Requires ARCore-supported device — not all phones
  • 🗓️Weeks of development — complex integration
  • 🔋High battery and CPU drain
  • 🎯Overkill — solves a different problem (3D overlay, not location check)
  • ❓Cannot verify site identity — only tracks relative movement

Our Implementation

How GPS Location Verification Works

1
Scan taken — GPS coordinates captured automatically and saved with the scan in the database
2
Worker opens result — app silently checks current GPS in the background
3
Distance calculated — Haversine formula compares current location vs scan location
4
If > 100m away — warning shown: "You are not at the scan location. Proceed with caution."

We only need to answer one question:
"Is this worker at the right site?"
GPS answers that perfectly. ARCore answers a completely different question.
